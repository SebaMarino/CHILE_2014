        We apply a multi-stage classification pipeline (see figure below) to the lightcurve data to isolate variable sources and identify candidates for follow up observation. First, we utilize the Stetson J index (Stetson 1996) to determine variability. For subsequent stages, we apply random forest classifiers with features derived from the lightcurves’ period and statistics, as well as color information from matched sources in various catalogs.
